Our Donation Plan

We are currently Collecting funds for the following services that are being provided by our volunteers to the affected people all around the country

Food for a family

Many people are unable to buy food for their family in disastrous situations. We give foods to those people in need.

Shelter for a family

Natural disasters destroy many homes and shelters. We provide safe and secure shelters to the affected people and their families.

education for a child

Disasters strike the education system pretty hard in the affected areas. Our well educated team members along with volunteers try to educate people about the safety and teach the children basic education.
